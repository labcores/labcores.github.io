LabCORES Projects

Year: 2016

Title: ZIKAlliance

Description: ZIKAlliance is a new international interdisciplinary consortium, led by the University of Marseille (France) which objective is to establish a multinational and multidisciplinary studies across the Latin America region, and to implement the urgently needed research during the ongoing ZIKV outbreak. It is formed of over 50 partners including Dr Alain Kohl (CVR), such as: Institut National de la Santé et de la Recherche Médicale (Inserm,France), Universitaetsklinikum Heidelberg(UKL-HD, Germany), Fundação Oswaldo Cruz (Fiocruz, Brazil), Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ, Brazil), Erasmus Medical Center (EMC, Netherlands), Leiden University Medical Center (LUMC, Netherlands), University of Leuven (KU Leuven , Belgium), Institut Pasteur Paris (IPP, France), University of Bonn Medical Centre (UKB, Germany), Institut de recherche pour le Developpment (IRD, France), Oxford University (UOXF, UK), Inserm Transfert (IT, France) and others.

Coordinator: Florence CHUNG
